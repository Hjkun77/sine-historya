welcome to

A curated catalog website of Philippine classical and historical film where students, teachers, and film enthusiasts can learn about Philippine history through film.

Films on this site can be streamed on several streaming websites or can be watched at various independent cinemas in Manila. We showcase films that cover topics about human rights, family dynamics, colonialism, and many more. We also offer educational learning modules that can enrich your knowledge on Philippine history through historical inquiry.

You’ll discover that the Philippine film industry has a rich selection of historical film that any age can enjoy.

Mission

Keep the local film industry alive

By watching films legally, you’re supporting the thousands of cast and crew members of the film industry to earn what is rightfully theirs.
